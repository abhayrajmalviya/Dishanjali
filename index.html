About Dishanjali

Dishanjali is a Non-Governmental organisation dedicated to the cause of helping underprivileged children educate themselves and promote the significance of education among the poor. With its aim to light up the lives of the needy children through the brightness of education, Dishanjali primarily engages in educating children who are willing to study yet are unable to educate themselves either due to scarcity of resources or unawareness. Enthusiastic and motivated youth actively participate in teaching these children to serve the greater purpose of bringing about a change in the society.
